You can use an RSS news reader to find out what is new in a Foswiki web. This complements WebChanges that shows recent changes in a web, and WebNotify where users can subscribe to get notified of recent changes by E-mail.

For the %USERSWEB% web, point your RSS news reader to:

<code> %SCRIPTURL{"view"}%/%USERSWEB%/WebRss </code>

For other Foswiki webs, replace =/%MAINWEB%/= with the name of the web:

%WEBLIST{ format="   * <code> %SCRIPTURL{"view"}%/$name/WebRss </code>" }%

See %SYSTEMWEB%.WebRssBase for more information on Foswiki RSS news feeds
